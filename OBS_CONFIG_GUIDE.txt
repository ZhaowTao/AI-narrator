========================================
OBS Audio Configuration Guide
========================================

1. Open OBS -> Settings -> Audio

Global Audio Devices:
  Desktop Audio: Your speakers/headphones
  Mic/Auxiliary Audio: Disabled

2. Click OK to save

========================================
Audio Flow Diagram
========================================

TTS Sound --> CABLE Input --> CABLE Output --> Doubao (hears prompt)
                                     ^
                                     |
                                  Listen --> Headphones (you hear)

Game Sound --> Speakers --> OBS captures --> Stream (viewers hear)
Doubao Voice --> Speakers --> OBS captures --> Stream (viewers hear)

KEY POINTS:
- TTS goes through CABLE, not through speakers
- You hear TTS through headphones via Listen feature
- OBS only captures speakers (game + doubao voice)
- Viewers CANNOT hear TTS prompts!

========================================
Checklist Before Streaming
========================================
[ ] TTS can play sound
[ ] Doubao can hear TTS (responds)
[ ] You can hear TTS through headphones
[ ] OBS does NOT capture TTS
[ ] OBS captures game sound
[ ] OBS captures doubao voice
